What we offer:

Air quality analytics

We provide air quality analytics with an easy to understand interpretation. Installations of air quality monitors can be limiting and the accuracy not guaranteed. That is why we make use of machine learning and satellite imagery to ensure we provide an accurate and localized information in real time including the emission sources and components of the pollutants . We make it accessible through mobile phones, smart watches and other portable devices.

Environmental data sets

Most environmental data used for development research and climate control is from Europe and other developed countries. Air pollution kills more African than any other major risk factor and yet a developing country like Nigeria accounts for a very low percentage of environmental data sets. We want to change this standard by making available, highly organized environmental data and analytics from Nigeria and Africa to be utilized locally and globally.

Research and development

We collaborate with researchers and institutions to promote environmental research and development that leads to ground-breaking innovations that can bring solutions that tackle some of the biggest challenges faced in our environment. Currently we are interested in research covering air pollution and sustainable environments, the activities that influence these changes in the environment and the consequences of these activities especially in the area of climate change and health.
